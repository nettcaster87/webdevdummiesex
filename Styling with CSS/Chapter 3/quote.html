Literature Quote of the Day

How to tell somebody off the classy way:

[Thine] horrid image doth unfix my hair.

William Shakespeare (Macbeth)

Emphasis and Strong Emphasis

This paragraph illustrates two main kinds of emphasis. This sentence uses the em tag. By default, the emphasis is italic. This sentence uses strong emphasis. The default formatting of strong emphasis is bold.

Of course you can change the formatting with CSS. This is a great example of semantic formatting. Rather than indication the formatting of some text, you indicate how much it is emphasised.

This way, you can go back and and change things, like adding color to emphasized text without the formatting commands muddying your actual text.
