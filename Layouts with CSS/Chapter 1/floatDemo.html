Float Demo

Paragraph 1. This paragraph has the normal behaviour of a block-level element. It takes up the entire width of the page, and the next element is placed underneath.

Paragraph 2. Adjusted the width of this paragraph to 50%. This parapgraph is floated left. It is palced to the left, and the next element will be placed to the right of it.

Paragraph 3. This paragraph has no floating width or margin. It takes whatever space it can to the right of the floated element and then flows to the next line.
